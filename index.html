Driving organizational success through leadership and innovation

With over 20 years of comprehensive experience in General Management, Financial and Human Resource Management, General Administration, Cost Management, Budgeting and Forecasting, Auditing, Taxation, and Software Operations Management, I am dedicated to driving organizational success and efficiency. My international studies have significantly broadened my horizons, providing a deep and nuanced understanding of global perspectives and practices. Pursuing a doctorate at Golden Gate University will further enrich this experience, allowing me to build a robust network of contacts and gain a profound insight into international business dynamics.

I excel at innovating and implementing solutions in complex environments, specializing in the restructuring and re-engineering of financial and administrative processes to maximize resource utilization. As a dynamic change agent, I have successfully repositioned organizations to achieve financial and operational excellence. I also lead software teams with exceptional skill—overseeing software operations end to end and aligning technical delivery with business goals. My ability to present financial data and operational performance insights empowers effective decision-making.

My extensive background also encompasses Human Resource Management & Compliance, Tactical Alliances, and Relationship Management. As a visionary leader with a keen eye for detail and a strong analytical aptitude, I address root causes and solve problems efficiently, driving success in both local and global contexts.
